No! This course is designed for complete beginners. We teach AI concepts first, then use visual tools and simple code. Your kids will learn at their own pace.
What age is this course for? ↓
The course is designed for ages 8-14. Younger kids (8-10) will need more parent guidance. Older kids (12-14) can work more independently. Adjustments can be made for different learning levels.
What tools and software do we need? ↓
Only a computer with internet and a modern web browser. All tools are free - no paid software needed. Everything runs in the browser, no installations required.
How long does the course take? ↓
The course is designed for 8 weeks at 2-3 hours per week. But it's self-paced! Your kids can move faster or slower based on their interests and schedule.

What happens after the course ends? ↓
Your child gets lifetime access to all materials with automatic updates. They can review lessons anytime, access the community, and earn their completion certificate.
Can this be used for a classroom? ↓
Yes! Teachers can use this for individual learning, group projects, or integrate it into their curriculum. We provide instructor guides and resources for educators.
