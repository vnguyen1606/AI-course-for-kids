# AI Glossary - Simple Explanations

A beginner-friendly glossary of AI and machine learning terms.

---

## A

**Artificial Intelligence (AI)**
Computer programs that can learn and make decisions, similar to how humans think.
*Example: ChatGPT, game opponents, photo filters*

**Algorithm**
A step-by-step set of rules that a computer follows to solve a problem.
*Example: Recipe for baking a cake*

---

## B

**Bias**
When an AI system favors certain groups unfairly.
*Example: A hiring AI that prefers men over women*

**Bot**
A computer program that performs tasks automatically.
*Example: A chatbot answering customer questions*

---

## C

**Classification**
Sorting things into groups or categories.
*Example: AI identifying if a photo contains a cat or dog*

**Computer Vision**
When computers learn to understand images and videos.
*Example: Face recognition, object detection*

---

## D

**Data**
Information used to train AI systems.
*Example: Thousands of photos labeled "cat" or "dog"*

**Decision Tree**
A way AI makes choices by asking yes/no questions.
*Example: "Is it a cat? If yes... If no..."*

---

## E

**Algorithm**
A set of instructions a computer follows.

---

## G

**Generative AI**
AI that creates new content (text, images, music).
*Example: DALL-E creating images, ChatGPT writing stories*

**GPU**
A powerful computer chip that helps AI learn faster.

---

## L

**Learning**
When AI improves by looking at examples.
*Example: AI learns what a dog looks like by seeing 1,000 dog pictures*

---

## M

**Machine Learning**
When computers learn from data instead of following pre-written rules.
*Example: Netflix learning your movie preferences*

**Model**
A trained AI system ready to make predictions or decisions.
*Example: A trained image recognition model*

---

## N

**Neural Network**
A type of AI inspired by how brains work, with connected layers.
*Example: Technology used in image recognition and language understanding*

**Natural Language Processing (NLP)**
When AI understands and responds to human language.
*Example: Chatbots, voice assistants, translation apps*

**NPC**
Non-Player Character - a character in a game controlled by AI.
*Example: Enemy in a video game*

---

## P

**Pattern**
A repeated rule or structure AI learns to recognize.
*Example: "Pictures with pointy ears + fur = cat"*

**Prediction**
When AI guesses what will happen based on patterns.
*Example: Predicting whether it will rain tomorrow*

---

## Q

**Query**
A question you ask an AI system.
*Example: "What's the weather today?" asked to a voice assistant*

---

## R

**Recognition**
When AI identifies something it's seen before.
*Example: Recognizing faces in photos*

---

## S

**Supervised Learning**
Teaching AI with labeled examples (the answers are provided).
*Example: Showing AI pictures labeled "cat" and "dog"*

---

## T

**Training**
The process of teaching an AI system using data.
*Example: Showing an AI 10,000 photos to learn what cats look like*

**Training Data**
The examples used to teach an AI system.
*Example: A collection of cat and dog photos*

---

## U

**Unsupervised Learning**
When AI finds patterns in data without being told what to look for.
*Example: AI grouping customers by shopping habits*

---

## V

**Vector**
A mathematical representation of data for AI.
*Example: Representing a photo as a list of numbers*

---

## W

**Weight**
A number that shows how important something is in AI decisions.
*Example: "Pointy ears" might have high weight for identifying cats*

---

## X-Z

**XAI (Explainable AI)**
AI systems that can explain why they made a decision.
*Example: "I identified this as spam because..."*

---

## Quick Reference

**Types of AI Learning:**
- **Supervised:** Learning from labeled examples
- **Unsupervised:** Finding patterns on its own
- **Reinforcement:** Learning through trial and error (like rewards and punishments)

**Common AI Applications:**
- **Recommendations:** Netflix, Spotify, Amazon
- **Recognition:** Face detection, voice commands
- **Generation:** ChatGPT, DALL-E, Midjourney
- **Gaming:** NPC behaviors, difficulty adjustment
- **Prediction:** Weather, stock prices, medical diagnosis
